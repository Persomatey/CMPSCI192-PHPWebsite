About

Software engineer based in Santa Clarita, CA. Working professionally in the games industry since 2015, I have worked in nearly all aspects of the industry from programming, to directing, level design, 3D modeling & animation, and quality assurance.

I have a Unity Programmer certification from Unity Technologies as well as a Computer Science degree from College of the Canyons where I joined the Computer Science Club in Spring 2017, and served as president of the club from Fall 2017 to Spring 2019 where we made a plethora of games.

While I’m not typing code, I’m mostly likely playing video games, tinkering with arduino projects, or shooting photography.
