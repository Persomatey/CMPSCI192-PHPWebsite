Legend of Fantasy
(COC CSC Spring 2019)

A top-down Zelda-like game with a fantasy theme. Fight your way through droves of villainous monsters such as orcs and mushrooms to make your way to the end to defeat the scourge of the Fantaswhere Forest, Orcutoryx.

As my last year as President of the Computer Science Club, I wanted to go out with a bang! I operated as director and lead programmer for this project. I worked on many aspects of the overall game. I programmed the core movement, health systems, animation rigging, boss mob behavior, and many other aspects of the game. You can see my name commented on pretty much every script.

Link to download: https://coc-computerscienceclub.itch.io/legend-of-fantasy
Link to GitHib repository: https://github.com/CanyonsCSClub/Spring2019

Space Shooter 9000
(COC CSC Fall 2017)

A side-scrolling space shooter arcade game with a low poly aesthetic. Battle through wave after wave of enemy ships as you plunge deep into enemy territory.

My first game as President of the Computer Science Club, we went for a simple arcade shooter. I operated as director and lead programmer for this project. I worked on nearly every aspect of the game. I programmed most of the enemy AI, the player projectiles, the enemy spawn system, the point and high score system, the health system, the unlock system, 3D modeling and animation, and many other aspects of the game. You can see my name commented on nearly every script in the game.

Link to download: https://coc-computerscienceclub.itch.io/space-shooter-9000
Link to GitHib repository: https://github.com/CanyonsCSClub/Fall2017

Factory
(COC CSC Fall 2016)

A top-down shooter with a military theme. Grind your way through hoards of zombies and droves of drones as you fight your way to the large boss drone at the end of the level to recapture the factory!

This was the first finished project that I worked on in Computer Science Club. I worked on the death state of the game.
